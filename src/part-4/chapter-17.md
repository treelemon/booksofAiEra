# Chapter 17: AI and the Public Sphere

## 17.1 Misinformation at Scale

AI enables synthetic media that is:
- **Cheap:** Generate thousands of variants per dollar
- **Personalized:** Targeted content for each viewer
- **Plausible:** Increasingly indistinguishable from reality
- **Viral:** AI-generated content exploits engagement algorithms

Election interference, financial scams, and social polarization are amplified.

## 17.2 Detection and Provenance

- **C2PA standard:** Cryptographic provenance metadata for content
- **Watermarking:** Invisible markers in AI-generated content (fragile, can be removed)
- **Detection AI:** Classifiers that detect AI generation (arms race)
- **Platform policies:** Labeling requirements for AI-generated political content

None of these is a silver bullet. The information environment will remain polluted for the foreseeable future.

## 17.3 AI in Governance

- **Estonia:** AI-powered public services (e-residency, tax filing)
- **Singapore:** AI for urban planning, traffic management, healthcare
- **China:** Social credit system, surveillance, predictive policing
- **US/UK:** Limited AI for document processing, fraud detection

The tension between efficiency and privacy/rights is unresolved.

## 17.4 AI in Warfare

- **Autonomous drones:** Ukraine conflict demonstrated AI-enabled targeting
- **Cyber operations:** AI-generated phishing, vulnerability discovery, defense
- **Decision support:** AI for intelligence analysis, logistics, strategy
- **Lethal autonomous weapons (LAWS):** No international treaty, growing concern

The UN has failed to reach consensus on autonomous weapons. The US, China, and Russia all oppose binding restrictions.

## 17.5 The Attention Economy

AI enables the most effective attention capture in human history:
- Personalized content feeds (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube)
- AI-generated content optimized for engagement
- Recommendation algorithms that maximize time spent

The human attention span is being optimized against. Awareness is the first line of defense.
