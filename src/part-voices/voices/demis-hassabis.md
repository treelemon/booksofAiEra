# Demis Hassabis

**Role:** CEO, Google DeepMind
**Active:** 2010–present
**Key contribution:** Co-founded DeepMind; led AlphaGo, AlphaFold, AlphaEvolve; pioneer of AI for scientific discovery

## Background

A former child chess prodigy and game designer, Hassabis co-founded DeepMind in 2010 with the mission of "solving intelligence." Google acquired DeepMind in 2014. Under his leadership, DeepMind has produced landmark results in games (AlphaGo), biology (AlphaFold), and algorithmic discovery (AlphaEvolve).

## Key Quotes

**On the mission (2017):**
> "We want to solve intelligence, and then use that to solve everything else."

**On AlphaFold (2021):**
> "This is the most significant contribution AI has made to the advancement of scientific knowledge. It's a breakthrough that I'm incredibly proud of."

**On AGI expectations (2024):**
> "The progress we've seen in the last few years is remarkable. But we're still missing some key pieces. I think we're on the right path, but there's genuinely hard work ahead."

**On the merger with Google Brain (2023):**
> "By bringing together Google Brain and DeepMind, under the Google DeepMind umbrella, we'll accelerate progress toward AGI and build more capable and responsible AI systems."

**On AI for discovery (2025, after AlphaEvolve):**
> "We're moving from AI that perceives and predicts to AI that creates and discovers. This is the beginning of a new era of AI-accelerated science."
