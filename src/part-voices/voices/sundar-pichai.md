# Sundar Pichai

**Role:** CEO, Alphabet (Google)
**Active:** 2004–present (CEO since 2015)
**Key contribution:** Led Google's AI-first transformation; launched Gemini (Google's most capable AI model family); integrated AI across Search, Cloud, Workspace, and Android

## Background

Pichai has been the driving force behind Google's pivot from "mobile-first" to "AI-first." Under his leadership, Google DeepMind (merged Google Brain + DeepMind) produces some of the world's leading AI models. He has consistently emphasized responsible AI development and the need for regulation.

## Key Quotes

**On AI as more profound than fire or electricity (2018):**
> "AI is one of the most profound things humanity is working on. It's more profound than fire or electricity."

**On responsible AI (2023, on Gemini launch):**
> "We are approaching AI with a deep sense of responsibility. We've published AI Principles, invested in safety research, and we're engaging with governments around the world on regulation."

**On the Gemini era (2024):**
> "Gemini represents our most capable and most responsible AI. It's multimodal, it's reasoning, and it's built from the ground up with safety in mind."

**On AI and Search (2024):**
> "Search will change more in the next five years than it has in the last twenty. AI-powered overviews, conversational search, multimodal queries — this is a fundamental shift."

**On regulation (2025):**
> "We need thoughtful regulation. Too much too early stifles innovation. Too little too late is dangerous. The balance is hard, but we need to find it."

**On progress (2026):**
> "We're still at the beginning. What we've achieved is remarkable, but the potential ahead is far greater. We're building AI that will help humanity solve its biggest challenges."
