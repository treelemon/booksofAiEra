# Jensen Huang

**Role:** Founder & CEO, NVIDIA
**Active:** 1993–present
**Key contribution:** Built the hardware that powers modern AI; transformed NVIDIA from a graphics company to the world's most valuable AI infrastructure provider

## Background

Huang co-founded NVIDIA in 1993. The company's GPUs proved ideal for neural network training, and the launch of CUDA (2006) enabled programmable GPU computing. By 2024, NVIDIA's market cap exceeded $3T as its hardware became the "picks and shovels" of the AI gold rush. Huang's annual GTC keynotes have become the most important events in the AI hardware calendar.

## Key Quotes

**On the iPhone moment (2023):**
> "ChatGPT is the iPhone moment of AI. It's the first time that AI has been put into the hands of everyone in a really useful and accessible way."

**On the new industrial revolution (2024):**
> "We are at the beginning of a new industrial revolution. This is not just about better chatbots. This is about the digitization of everything — every factory, every supply chain, every industry will be transformed."

**On Moore's Law and AI (2024):**
> "Moore's Law is dead. But the rate of AI improvement is far outpacing Moore's Law. We are doubling the capability of our chips every year, and we will continue to do so."

**On software being eaten by AI (2025):**
> "The traditional way of writing software is over. We are now in the era of AI-driven software. Every application will be infused with intelligence. The next trillion-dollar company will be an AI company."

**On the longevity of demand (2025):**
> "The demand for our GPUs is not just from training. Inference is going to be enormous. Every time you use an AI, you're using a GPU. That demand will continue to grow for years."

**On AGI (2026):**
> "Within the next five years, we'll see systems that can pass any test a human can pass. The question isn't if, but when. And we're building the infrastructure for that world right now."
