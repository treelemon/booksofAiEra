# Satya Nadella

**Role:** CEO, Microsoft
**Active:** 2014–present
**Key contribution:** Transformed Microsoft around AI; orchestrated multi-billion dollar investment in OpenAI; integrated AI across Azure, Office, Windows, and Bing

## Background

Nadella became Microsoft CEO in 2014 and shifted the company from "Windows-first" to "cloud-first." His decision to invest deeply in OpenAI (starting with $1B in 2019, scaling to over $13B) positioned Microsoft at the center of the AI revolution. Under his leadership, Microsoft launched Copilot across all products and became the primary cloud provider for OpenAI.

## Key Quotes

**On the AI platform shift (2023):**
> "This is a platform shift. It's as significant as the PC, the web, and the smartphone. It will change how people work, create, and interact."

**On the OpenAI partnership (2023):**
> "We have a very deep partnership that is built on trust and shared mission. We're not just investors — we're building together."

**On democratizing AI (2024):**
> "Our goal is to put AI in the hands of every person and every organization on the planet. Not just the Fortune 500, but every small business, every non-profit, every student."

**On AI's impact on work (2024):**
> "Copilot is not about replacing people. It's about freeing people from drudgery so they can focus on what they're best at: creativity, collaboration, and complex problem-solving."

**On responsible AI (2025):**
> "We have a responsibility to build AI that is safe, secure, and trustworthy. That means investing in safety research, building guardrails, and engaging with policymakers."

**On AI agents (2026):**
> "Agents will be the next big thing. They don't just respond to prompts — they act on your behalf. This is a fundamental shift from copilot to autonomous assistance."
