# Aiden Gomez

**Role:** Co-Founder & CEO, Cohere
**Active:** 2019–present
**Key contribution:** Co-authored the "Attention Is All You Need" paper (2017) as a Google Brain intern; founded Cohere to build enterprise AI; leading voice for practical, secure, enterprise-grade AI

## Background

Gomez was one of eight authors of the landmark "Attention Is All You Need" paper (2017) — as an intern at Google Brain. He co-founded Cohere in 2019 to build AI for enterprise, focusing on retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), data privacy, and secure deployment. Cohere has become a leading enterprise AI platform.

## Key Quotes

**On the transformer paper (2023):**
> "We had no idea it would change the world. We were just trying to solve a technical problem in machine translation. The fact that it became the foundation of modern AI still surprises me."

**On enterprise AI (2024):**
> "Consumer AI is about entertainment and general assistance. Enterprise AI is about accuracy, security, and integration with existing systems. These are fundamentally different requirements."

**On RAG (2024):**
> "Retrieval-augmented generation is the key to making AI reliable for business. If you connect the model to your data, you ground it in facts. You solve hallucination not by making the model better — but by giving it the right information."

**On data privacy (2025):**
> "Enterprises will not put their data into a black box. They need to know their data is secure, that it's not being used to train the next model, and that they have control. That's what we built Cohere for."

**On the future (2025):**
> "We're moving from chatbots to systems that actually do work. The next five years will be about making AI reliable enough to trust with important tasks."

**On competition (2026):**
> "The AI market is big enough for many winners. We don't need to beat OpenAI or Anthropic at everything. We need to be the best at serving enterprise needs."
