# Dario Amodei

**Role:** CEO, Anthropic
**Active:** 2021–present
**Key contribution:** Founded Anthropic after leaving OpenAI; pioneered Constitutional AI; built the Claude model family with safety as the first priority

## Background

Amodei was VP of Research at OpenAI before leaving in 2021 to founded Anthropic with a group of former OpenAI researchers. Anthropic's mission: build safe, capable AI systems through rigorous alignment research. Claude has become one of the leading frontier model families, with a reputation for safety and reliability.

## Key Quotes

**On why he left OpenAI to start Anthropic (2023):**
> "The most important thing for the future of AI is to solve the safety problem. Starting a new company that's built around that mission from day one was the best way to do it."

**On Constitutional AI (2023):**
> "Instead of relying solely on human feedback, we wanted models to have an internal constitution — a set of principles they use to evaluate their own outputs. It's more scalable and principled."

**On the stakes (2024):**
> "I think there's a real chance that AI systems will be more capable than humans in most domains within the next decade. Getting the alignment problem right before that happens is the most important technical challenge of our time."

**On Claude's approach (2025):**
> "We're building Claude to be helpful, honest, and harmless. Not because it's the easiest thing to do, but because we think that's what AI should be. Capability without safety is not a product — it's a liability."

**On competition driving safety risks (2025):**
> "The competitive pressure to ship more capable systems creates a natural incentive to cut corners on safety. We need structural solutions — regulation, standards, independent auditing — to counteract that."
