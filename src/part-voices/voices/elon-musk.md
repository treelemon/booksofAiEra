# Elon Musk

**Role:** CEO, xAI / Tesla / SpaceX
**Active:** 2015–present (xAI: 2023–present)
**Key contribution:** Co-founded OpenAI (2015, left 2018); founded xAI (2023, Grok); pushed AI for autonomous driving and robotics

## Background

Musk has been a central and controversial figure in AI from the beginning. He co-founded OpenAI as a counterweight to Google's AI dominance, left after disagreements over direction, and later founded xAI to build what he calls "maximum truth-seeking AI." His comments on AI risk have oscillated between existential warnings and active acceleration.

## Key Quotes

**On AI as existential risk (2014):**
> "We need to be very careful with artificial intelligence. I think we need to be proactive about regulation rather than reactive. I think by the time we are reactive, it's too late."

**On leaving OpenAI (2023):**
> "I originally created OpenAI as an open-source, non-profit organization. It is now a closed-source, for-profit organization. That was not my intent."

**On founding xAI (2023):**
> "The goal of xAI is to understand the true nature of the universe. We want to build an AI that is maximally curious and truth-seeking."

**On AGI timeline (2024):**
> "I think we'll have AGI probably by 2026 or 2027. That might seem optimistic, but the rate of improvement is just staggering."

**On autonomous driving (2025):**
> "Full self-driving is finally real. The neural network can now drive better than a human. This year, Tesla will have more autonomous miles than all other cars combined."

**On competition (2025, on Grok vs ChatGPT):**
> "Grok is designed to answer the spicy questions that other AI models won't touch. It should be helpful to humanity, not politically correct."
