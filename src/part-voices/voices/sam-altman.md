# Sam Altman

**Role:** CEO, OpenAI
**Active:** 2015–present
**Key contribution:** Led OpenAI from non-profit research lab to the world's most valuable AI company; launched ChatGPT, GPT-4, DALL-E, reasoning models

## Background

Altman was president of Y Combinator before joining OpenAI as CEO in 2019. Under his leadership, OpenAI shifted from a non-profit to a capped-profit structure and became the defining company of the generative AI boom.

## Key Quotes

**On AI as infrastructure (2023):**
> "AI is going to be the most powerful technology humanity has ever created. It's going to be like the transistor — it's going to be something that underlies pretty much everything we do."

**On the pace of progress (2024):**
> "I think people underestimate the speed at which this is happening. The capabilities are increasing faster than most people realize."

**On AGI timeline (2025):**
> "I think we will have systems that are broadly capable of doing any cognitive work that a human can do, probably sooner than most people expect. The societal adjustment is the harder part."

**On safety (2023, Senate testimony):**
> "If this technology goes wrong, it can go quite wrong. We try to be very clear-eyed about that. We need regulatory intervention to make sure this is done safely."

**On open-source (2025, after releasing an open model):**
> "We've learned that the ecosystem is healthier when there's a mix of open and closed. The world benefits from both approaches."
