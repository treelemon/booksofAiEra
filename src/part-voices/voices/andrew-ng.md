# Andrew Ng

**Role:** Founder, DeepLearning.AI; Founder, AI Fund; Former Chief Scientist, Baidu
**Active:** 2000s–present
**Key contribution:** Pioneered online AI education (Coursera ML course, DeepLearning.AI); led Google Brain and Baidu AI; built the first AI education pipeline for millions

## Background

Ng co-founded Google Brain (2011) and later led Baidu's AI group (2014–2017). His Coursera Machine Learning course (2012) introduced hundreds of thousands to AI. He founded DeepLearning.AI (2017) to scale AI education globally and AI Fund to incubate AI companies. He is the most influential AI educator in the world.

## Key Quotes

**On AI as the new electricity (2017):**
> "AI is the new electricity. Just as electricity transformed industry after industry starting about 100 years ago, AI will transform every major industry."

**On the skills gap (2019):**
> "AI is a new kind of literacy. In the 21st century, understanding the basics of AI is as important as understanding how to use a computer. We need to teach everyone."

**On education at scale (2023):**
> "We've taught millions of people AI through online courses. But the demand is still far greater than the supply. We need to train not just AI engineers, but everyone — marketers, doctors, farmers — on how to use AI."

**On AI's value (2024):**
> "The most valuable AI projects often aren't the most technically impressive ones. They're the ones that solve real problems for real businesses with high-quality data."

**On agentic AI (2025):**
> "Agentic AI is the biggest trend of 2025. We're moving from chat to action. The ability to build systems that plan, use tools, and execute tasks is a fundamental shift."

**On AI not replacing jobs (2026):**
> "I believe AI will not replace most jobs. But people who use AI will replace people who don't. The skill is not competing with AI — it's collaborating with it."
