# Mark Zuckerberg

**Role:** CEO, Meta
**Active:** 2004–present
**Key contribution:** Pivoted Meta to be an AI-first company; led the release of the open-source Llama model family; invested heavily in AI infrastructure and AI-powered products

## Background

Zuckerberg was relatively late to the AI race compared to Google and Microsoft, but Meta has since become one of the most aggressive AI companies. Meta's Llama models have become the leading open-weight models, and Zuckerberg has positioned Meta behind open-source AI as a strategic bet.

## Key Quotes

**On pivoting to AI (2023):**
> "We are now an AI company. Our recommendation systems, our ads, our content moderation — all powered by AI. And we're building the next generation of AI for the metaverse."

**On open-source (2024, on Llama 3):**
> "Open-source AI is the best path forward. It creates the most value for the most people. It's safer because it's transparent. And it ensures that the benefits of AI are widely distributed."

**On the Llama strategy (2024):**
> "Llama won't be the most powerful model at any given moment. But it will be the most accessible. And over time, the open-source ecosystem will catch up and surpass closed models."

**On AI infrastructure (2025):**
> "We're spending tens of billions on AI infrastructure this year — GPUs, data centers, energy. This is the most important investment Meta has ever made."

**On AI agents (2025):**
> "We're building AI agents that can interact with customers, create content, and help people in their daily lives. Hundreds of thousands of businesses are already using our AI tools."

**On competition (2026):**
> "The AI race is not a winner-take-all market. There will be many successful AI platforms. Our bet is on openness, scalability, and integration with the social graph."
