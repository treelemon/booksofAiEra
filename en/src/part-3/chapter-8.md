# Chapter 8: Multimodal and Embodied AI

## 8.1 The Multimodal Shift

Humans experience the world through multiple senses. Until 2023, AI handled text, images, and audio separately. Today's frontier models handle all modalities natively.

**Current capabilities:**
- **Text + images:** GPT-4o, Gemini, Claude, Llama 5
- **+ Audio:** Real-time speech in/out (GPT-4o, Gemini)
- **+ Video:** Understanding and generation (Sora, Gemini, Kling)
- **+ Code execution:** Integrated interpreter (GPT-4o, Claude)

## 8.3 Robotics and Embodiment

AI is moving beyond text and images into physical action:
- **Tesla Optimus / Figure 02:** humanoid robots with foundation model brains
- **Mobile ALOHA / π0:** learned manipulation policies
- **RT-2 / RT-X:** vision-language-action models

**Key insight:** Embodiment grounds AI in physics. This may be necessary (though not sufficient) for robust intelligence.

## 8.4 Edge AI and On-Device Multimodality

By 2026, many AI capabilities run locally:
- Apple Intelligence: on-device LLM + image generation
- Samsung Galaxy AI: real-time translation, photo editing
- Google Pixel: on-device Gemini Nano

Privacy and latency drive this trend. Most queries will shift to hybrid (edge + cloud) within 2–3 years.
