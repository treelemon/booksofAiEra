## Essential Papers

- [**Attention Is All You Need**](https://arxiv.org/abs/1706.03762) (Vaswani et al., 2017) — The transformer paper
- [**Scaling Laws for Neural Language Models**](https://arxiv.org/abs/2001.08361) (Kaplan et al., 2020) — Why scale works
- [**Training Language Models to Follow Instructions with RLHF**](https://arxiv.org/abs/2203.02155) (Ouyang et al., 2022) — InstructGPT
- [**Chain-of-Thought Prompting**](https://arxiv.org/abs/2201.11903) (Wei et al., 2022) — Reasoning breakthrough
- [**Constitutional AI**](https://arxiv.org/abs/2212.08073) (Bai et al., 2022) — Anthropic's alignment approach
- [**DeepSeek-R1**](https://arxiv.org/abs/2501.12948) (2025) — Open-source reasoning model
- [**The WMDP Benchmark**](https://arxiv.org/abs/2403.03235) (2024) — Measuring dangerous capability
