# Foreword

*Why this book exists, and why now.*

I started tracking AI seriously in 2022. Back then, GPT-3 had just demonstrated that scaling language models produced capabilities no one had predicted — and the field was still debating whether it was a breakthrough or a curiosity. What I didn't realize was that I was watching the early tremors of a tectonic shift.

This book is anchored by a single conviction — **Human Wisdom**: humanity at the center, intelligence as the instrument.

The AI era will tempt you with two opposite errors: to worship machines as oracles, or to dismiss them as toys. Both miss the mark. The truth is simpler and more profound: **you already have what matters most.** Your judgment, your values, your lived experience — these are not obsolete. They are the foundation. AI is a tool, a partner, a mirror. It reflects and amplifies human wisdom, but it does not replace it.

This book is the result of years of watching, building, and thinking about artificial intelligence. It's not a prediction — predictions in this field age in hours. It's a framework: a set of mental models, historical context, and technical understanding that will let you navigate whatever comes next — with your own wisdom as the compass.

I wrote this as a living document because AI doesn't stand still. Every chapter will be updated as the landscape shifts. My goal is to make this the single best resource for understanding the AI era — not as a static reference, but as a continuously updated field guide.

If you're reading this, you're probably already feeling the acceleration. Let's understand it together.
